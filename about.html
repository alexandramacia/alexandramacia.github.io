Alexandra Macia is a senior journalism and criminal justice double major at the University of Maryland, College Park. Over the years Alexandra has been involved in a variety of on campus publications including The Campus Trainer, The Mitzpeh and Unwind Magazine. She has also in recent years interned at a diverse set of news organizations including Voice of America, The Zebra Press and Montgomery Community Media.

Alexandra's experience has included writing news articles, producing and hosting live shows and shooting and editing video footage/content. Some of the platforms Alexandra has experience with include Audition, Premiere and Wordpress. She is a quicklearner and can easily adapt to any given situation.

During the fall semester of 2020, Alexandra helped Voice of America cover the 2020 presidential election. Her work included speaking with people all over the nation about issues regarding the election. While Alexandra has experience with interviewing people on a national level, she also has experience working at a small local newspaper, such as The Zebra Press. Her diverse background has helped her to grow as a journalist and develop a wideset of skills.
